About The Event

EVOLVAI 2K26 is a futuristic, AI-driven national level technical fest organized by JOY UNIVERSITY. The event serves as a dynamic platform where innovation meets intelligence, creativity aligns with technology, and talent transforms into excellence. Bringing together students, professionals, and industry experts, EVOLVAI 2K26 celebrates Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Databases, Web Technologies, Competitive Gaming, and Creative Thinking through immersive competitions and knowledge-driven challenges.
